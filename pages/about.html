About Our Solution

Purpose of the Solution

Our financial management software aims to assist small businesses in Denmark with predictive budgeting tools. It enables timely and informed financial decision-making by providing insights into future financial performance.

Calculations

The software uses advanced algorithms to analyze past financial data and project future trends. Key calculations include:

  • Revenue and expense forecasting
  • Profit and loss predictions
  • Cash flow projections
  • Budget variance analysis

How to Use

To use the solution effectively:

  1. Upload historical financial data or input data manually.
  2. Define business goals and planned deviations.
  3. Review predictive budget reports and adjust strategies accordingly.
  4. Monitor real-time updates and adjust for deviations in performance.
